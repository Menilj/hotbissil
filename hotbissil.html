About Our Church

Transforming Lives in Kajiado

House of Transformation (HOT) is a vibrant Christian community located in Bissil, Kajiado Central, Kenya. Founded in 2019, we are committed to spreading the Gospel and transforming lives through the power of God's word.

Our mission is to transform the lives of God’s people for spiritual and physical prosperity by Preaching the Kingdom of God and those things which concern the Lord Jesus Christ. (Acts 28:31)

Our Vision is to see the church flourishing and holistically transforming people’s lives through the anointed Gospel globally. (Matthew 28:19–20)
